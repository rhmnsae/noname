Our Story

The Secret Ingredient
is Time.

We believe that the finest cakes are never rushed. From sourcing free-range eggs at the local market to allowing our signature sponge to rest overnight — every decision at Oven & Crumbs is intentional and deliberate.

Born in a small home kitchen in Bandung in 1998, we have grown into a beloved artisan bakery without ever abandoning the soul of that original kitchen: honesty, quality, and the kind of warmth that only handmade food can carry.

How We Bake

From Source to Shelf.

01

Sourcing

We work directly with local farmers for fresh eggs, pure butter, and seasonal produce. No compromises on provenance.

02

Preparation

Each recipe is prepared by hand. Doughs are mixed, laminated, or folded according to their own logic — never rushed by machine.

03

Baking

Our deck ovens are fired from 04:00 every morning. Temperature, humidity, and timing are all monitored by the baker — not a timer.

04

Delivery

Same-day delivery within Bandung. Pre-orders are packed with care in our signature kraft boxes and dispatched before noon.
