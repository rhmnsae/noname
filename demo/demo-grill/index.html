Our Philosophy

How We Work

FROM RANCH to your table. TO PLATE

The journey from raw cut to extraordinary plate is a series of deliberate, irreplaceable steps. Nothing is rushed. Nothing is compromised.

01

Selection

Only USDA Prime grade beef from heritage ranches, graded in the top 2% of all cattle raised in the country.

02

Dry Aging

A minimum of 45 days in our in-house temperature-controlled aging room. Enzymatic breakdown concentrates flavor and breaks down muscle fiber.

03

Preparation

Hand-butchered to spec. Seasoned simply with Himalayan salt and cracked pepper. The quality of the meat requires nothing more.

04

The Fire

Cooked over 800-degree custom iron grates over white oak and hickory. A deep caramelized crust forms within seconds. Perfection follows.

"A great steak is not cooked. It is orchestrated."

Marco Reyes, Executive Chef
